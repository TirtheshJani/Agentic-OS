# Curated ML handles

Default watchlist for `ml-twitter-watch`. Override at call time via the
`handles` input. Kept small and reputable; quality over coverage.

| Handle | Why watch |
|---|---|
| `@karpathy` | Practitioner-grade explanations of training, tokenization, scaling; high signal threads. |
| `@ylecun` | Frequent disagreements on architecture and AGI framing; surfaces debates. |
| `@hardmaru` (David Ha) | Evolutionary methods, world models, generative art research; curates papers. |
| `@_jasonwei` | Author of chain-of-thought; posts research takes and paper roundups. |
| `@sama` | OpenAI direction signals; product and policy framing. |
| `@jackclarkSF` | Anthropic policy lead; Import AI newsletter author; weekly trend framing. |
| `@soumithchintala` | PyTorch lead; systems/infra perspective on training stacks. |
| `@hyhieu226` (Hieu Pham) | Pretraining and post-training research notes from Google/DeepMind alumni circle. |
| `@hwchung27` (Hyung Won Chung) | Scaling-laws and instruction-tuning commentary. |
| `@demishassabis` | DeepMind announcements; rare but high-signal posts on Gemini / AlphaFold lineage. |

## Notes

- Handles change names and accounts go private. If a fetch returns 404 or
  a redirected login page, drop the handle for that run and record it
  under the digest's "Notes" section.
- Add domain-specific handles (physics-ML, mech-interp, RL) by appending
  rows here. Keep total under ~20 to stay within a sensible token budget.
